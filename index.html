Combat Robotics

What we do

Combat Robotics, also known as Battlebots, is a type of robotics competition in which robots are designed, built, and fought against each other. We primarily compete in local Combat Robotics competitions, most of which follow the Standardized procedures for the Advancement of Robot Combat (SPARC) ruleset.
